We are advocates for both the advancement and development of sports. Craftsmanship, or “monozukuri”, and innovation are the words that describe Yonex’s core practices.


By combining timeless Japanese craftsmanship and cutting-edge technology, we have been giving players the tools they need to triumph on the court for over 60 years.

Racqets come in range of soft feeling (flexible shaft) to solid feeling (stiff shaft) to fulfil individual player's needs during matches. On the other hand, racqets with different balance points from head-light to head-heavy, is another point to consider during racqet selection.


Selecting a racqet by putting the 2 features above together would result in player finding the perfect racqet.
